📊 Project Overview
This Power BI project provides a comprehensive analysis of marketing campaign performance across multiple products, regions, and digital channels. By integrating financial metrics with engagement data, the dashboard enables stakeholders to evaluate the efficiency of marketing spend and identify high-growth opportunities.

❓ Problem Statement
Marketing teams often struggle to consolidate data from fragmented sources (Email, Social Media, Search Engines, and Direct Mail). Without a unified view, it is difficult to:

Identify which channels yield the highest Return on Investment (ROI).

Understand regional variations in product demand.

Track conversion trends over specific fiscal quarters.

Determine if the current Cost Per Click (CPC) is sustainable relative to revenue.

🎯 Purpose & Objectives
The primary purpose of this dashboard is to transition from raw data to actionable marketing intelligence.

Performance Tracking: Monitor core KPIs including Total Revenue ($1.04M), Spend ($261.17K), and Average ROI (4.48).

Channel Optimization: Compare revenue vs. spend across channels to reallocate budgets toward high-performing platforms.

Regional Strategy: Analyze "Product Conversions by Region" to tailor localized marketing efforts.

Product Analysis: Visualize product contribution to total revenue (e.g., Product C leading at 33.11%).

🛠️ Data Stack & Tools
Power BI Desktop: Used for data modeling, DAX calculations, and visualization.

Power Query: Employed for ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes to clean and shape the data.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Used to create custom measures like Average ROI, CTR, and CPC.

🔍 Key Insights from the Dashboard
High Efficiency: The East and West regions are dominant drivers of conversions, specifically for Product C.

Channel Leaders: While Email and Social Media drive significant volume, the "Total Revenue & Spend by Channel" chart helps identify which channel has the lowest acquisition cost.

Revenue Seasonality: The "Revenue by Quarter & Region" area chart reveals fluctuations, showing a notable peak in the West region during Q4.
